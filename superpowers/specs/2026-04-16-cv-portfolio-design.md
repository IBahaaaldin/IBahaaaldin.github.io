## Deliverable 3: Client Acquisition Strategy

### Track A — Freelance: UAE Restaurants & SMBs

**Channel 1: Instagram (highest ROI)**
- Post "before/after" reels of restaurant websites in Arabic
- Target: Restaurant owners in Dubai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi
- Content ideas: bilingual toggle demo, admin dashboard walkthrough, menu system showcase
- Hook copy (Arabic): *"هل مطعمك محتاج موقع؟"* (Does your restaurant need a website?)

**Channel 2: Google Maps Prospecting**
- Search "restaurants in [Dubai/Sharjah area]" on Google Maps
- Find ones with no website or a poor one
- Contact via WhatsApp or Instagram DM
- Opening message: *"Hi, I'm Bahaa — I built Hamasat Burger's website [hamasatburger.com]. I'd love to do something similar for you."*
- Expected: 1 in 10 responds → 1 in 3 becomes client → 30 messages = 1 new client

**Channel 3: Referrals from Existing 26 Clients (easiest win)**
- Message each existing client: *"Do you know any other restaurants that need a website?"*
- Incentive: *"Refer someone who becomes a client, I'll give you a free feature upgrade."*
- Ask for WhatsApp voice note testimonial for social proof
- Expected: 5–10 warm leads within 2 weeks

**Channel 4: Upwork (international)**
- Profile headline: *"Full-Stack Developer | Marketplace & Restaurant Platforms | AI-Augmented | Bilingual EN/AR"*
- Niche: marketplace development + bilingual Arabic/English websites
- Starting rate: $25–35/hr (builds reviews fast)
- First bids: "restaurant website" and "marketplace development" jobs only

### Track B — Employment: UAE Tech Companies

**Platforms:**
- LinkedIn (primary) — rewrite profile to match new CV
- Bayt.com — UAE's #1 job portal, set job alerts
- GulfTalent — mid-senior Gulf tech market
- Direct email to hiring managers via LinkedIn

**Target companies:** Property Finder, Noon, Careem, Talabat, Dubizzle, UAE startups

**LinkedIn profile rewrites:**
- Headline: `Full-Stack Developer & AI-Augmented Engineer | Marketplace Platforms | UAE`
- About: Mirror new CV summary — lead with the numbers
- Featured: Pin CarzUAE, Designers Puzzle, portfolio link
